How we help

Civic First are establishing a new talent pipeline to attract brilliant and inspirational participants from different backgrounds, and support them to become the public leaders we deserve.

Our Fellowship programme equips passionate individuals who are looking to make a difference, with the skills they need to navigate and succeed in the realm of politics and public life.

What we do

The Fellowship programme is a unique year-long programme which has been designed to be conducted alongside a full-time job, and is offered complete free. Spanning three carefully structured terms of ten weeks each, the programme convenes for discussion on a weekday evening in central London from October to July.

At the end of the programme, we pair you with a senior mentor already working in public life and we support in transitioning to opportunities in that sphere.

Who we are looking for

For too long, politics and public life has been dominated by the wrong kinds of politicians, we're here to fix that.

Our Fellowship programme is open to people from a wide-variety of backgrounds, for example, you might be:

  • An involved member of your local community who gets involved in initiatives
  • A committed individual who's felt firsthand the current issues affecting people, and are passionate about taking action to fix them
  • Or, you might already be working in the public sector
